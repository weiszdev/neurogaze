NeuroGaze

Functional Saccadic Assessment Protocol

This tool uses your camera to track eye movements across 10 clinical gaze zones. No video is recorded. All data stays on your device.

1
Camera Setup — Allow webcam access so we can track your eye movements.
2
Calibration — Click 9 dots on screen to train the eye tracker to your eyes.
3
10 Gaze Zones — A dot appears in different positions. Look at it and hold your gaze for 5 seconds per zone.
4
Feedback — After each zone, report any difficulty or symptoms you experienced.
5
Report — Get a detailed breakdown of your saccadic performance with exercise recommendations.
This is a screening tool, not a medical diagnosis. Consult a licensed practitioner for clinical interpretation.
For best results, use a laptop or desktop with a webcam. Mobile devices are not ideal for eye tracking.
